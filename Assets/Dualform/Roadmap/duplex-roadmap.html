Chapter 01

Why duplexes outperform

Most investors own a single rental property. It earns one income, carries one vacancy risk, and builds equity at whatever pace the market sets. A duplex changes that equation — and the difference, when you look at it side by side, is significant.

Two dwellings. Two independent incomes. One parcel of land — and one managed process.

Standard Buy & Hold Duplex Development
Income streams One rental Two independent rentals
Typical gross yield 3.5 – 4.5% 5.5 – 7.0%
Vacancy exposure Full impact on cashflow — one empty means zero income Distributed — one tenanted dwelling still generates income
Equity at completion Market appreciation only Development margin built in from day one
Subdivision potential Rarely applicable Torrens or strata title — two separately sellable assets
Portfolio leverage One asset, one equity position Two assets, two valuations, accelerated equity base
Management pathway Self-managed or agent only End-to-end with Dualform — land to lease

Chapter 04

Finance & build

The two questions every investor asks first: how do I fund it, and what actually happens during the build? Here's the straightforward answer to both.

Finance pathways
Most common

Construction Loan

Land is purchased first, then a construction loan draws down in stages as the build progresses. You only pay interest on what's been released — keeping holding costs controlled through the build period.

Existing property owners

Equity Release

If you own property with usable equity, that equity can serve as your deposit — keeping cash reserves intact while the asset base expands. Our lending team will map your current equity position in your first session.

Super-funded investment

SMSF

Self-managed super funds can access duplex development under the right structure. We work with SMSF-specialist lenders across our panel and can refer you to an SMSF-qualified adviser as part of the process.

Shared capital

Joint Venture

Co-investing with a partner can reduce individual capital requirements while preserving access to the same development returns. JV arrangements are common for investors building toward larger portfolio positions.

The 10-stage build process

From first conversation to final lease

  1. 01

    Strategy & Financial Assessment

    We map your borrowing capacity, equity position, and investment goals. This shapes everything that follows and costs nothing.

  2. 02

    Site Selection & Due Diligence

    We source land that meets duplex development criteria — zoning, dimensions, council requirements. Every site is reviewed before it reaches you.

  3. 03

    Design & Town Planning

    Our team works with town planners to prepare compliant designs and lodgement-ready documentation for the DA process.

  4. 04

    Development Approval

    DA lodgement through council or private certifier. Typical approval timeline of 6–10 weeks. We manage this process — you're kept informed at each milestone.

  5. 05

    Finance Approval

    Formal finance approval is sought through our lender panel. Construction loan conditions are confirmed before contracts are executed.

  6. 06

    Land Settlement

    Contracts are executed and land settles. Your build contract with the fixed price is confirmed at this stage.

  7. 07

    Construction Commencement

    Builder mobilises within days of receiving the initial progress payment. Construction typically runs 22–26 weeks from this point.

  8. 08

    Progress Inspections

    Independent third-party inspections at each build milestone — slab, frame, lock-up, fit-out, practical completion. Photo documentation at every stage.

  9. 09

    Completion, Handover & Titles

    Final inspection before the last progress payment is released. Torrens or strata titles are registered — two separately titled assets from one project.

  10. 10

    Rental Setup, Review & Next Project

    Both dwellings are leased through our property management network. We then review your new equity position and plan the next step in your portfolio.

Chapter 05

The Dualform model

The phrase "done for you" gets used loosely in property investment. Here's what it specifically means when you work with Dualform — and what you don't have to touch.

01

Site sourcing, not site searching

We identify and review sites before they're presented to you. Every project has already passed our internal due diligence — zoning, dimensions, estimated build costs, and projected returns — before you see a number.

02

Fixed-price, fully turnkey contracts

Your construction price is locked before you commit. No variations, no unexpected additions at stage four. The number you see at the start is the number you build to.

03

Access to a specialist lending panel

Our finance team works with 30+ bank and non-bank lenders with experience in duplex construction lending. We handle the application and manage the drawdown schedule during the build.

04

Single point of contact, entire project

Planning, approvals, builder coordination, inspections — one team manages it all. You're not the project manager. You're kept informed; we handle the complexity.

05

Independent inspections at every milestone

Third-party inspectors — not our own team — review the build at each progress stage. You receive photographic reports. The final payment is only released after the completion inspection is clear.

06

Post-settlement strategy review

When the project is complete, we don't disappear. We review your new equity position, new borrowing capacity, and discuss what the next project in your portfolio looks like from here.

What you handle: reviewing project information, making decisions, and signing off at key milestones. That's the involvement required. Everything else — sourcing, planning, building, leasing — is managed on your behalf.
