Common questions

Answered plainly.

A duplex is two dwellings on a single parcel of land — either side by side or front and back. Each dwelling has its own entrance, living areas, and utilities, and can be leased to separate tenants. This gives you two income streams from one site, and depending on the structure, the ability to hold them under individual Torrens titles as two separate assets.
Typical Dualform projects range from $900,000 to $1.2M total (land + build + costs). The deposit and equity requirements depend on your existing asset position and finance structure. Our finance team will assess your borrowing capacity in your first strategy call — at no cost and with no commitment required. Investors with $150k–$250k+ in usable equity or cash are often well positioned to proceed.

From your first strategy call to both dwellings tenanted, a typical Dualform project runs 9–14 months. The build itself takes approximately 22–26 weeks from commencement. Development approval (DA) typically takes 6–12 weeks depending on the state and council. Timeframes can vary based on site complexity and market conditions.
