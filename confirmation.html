How to get the most from your call

Four things worth knowing before you dial in.

Finance

Know your borrowing capacity (roughly)

Even a ballpark figure from your bank or broker helps us tailor the conversation to what's actually available to you.

Goals

Be clear on what matters most to you

Is it monthly cashflow, long-term equity, or both? The clearer you are, the more specific we can be about which projects fit.

Questions

Bring a list — nothing is off limits

Write down every question you have, including the ones you think might be too basic. That's what the call is for.

Logistics

Be somewhere you can speak freely

A private space with a good phone signal makes a big difference. The call will run exactly 30 minutes unless you'd like more time.
